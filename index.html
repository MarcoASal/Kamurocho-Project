Top Three activities to do in Kamurocho

Take a swing!

Visit Yoshida Batting Center to unwind after a long day and try the hitting challenges to earn prizes!

Play some games!

Visit Club Sega and enjoy some arcade games! They also have photo booths and UFO Catchers!

See Kamurocho's most famous sight!

Millennium Tower is the first place you think of when you hear Kamurocho! So come see it yourself!
